Rules :

1. The game is played on a grid that's 20 squares by 20 squares.
2. Player 1 is X, Player 2 is O. Players take turns putting their marks in empty squares.
3. The first player to get 5 of their marks in a row (up, down, across, or diagonally) gets a point.
4. The board is reset after each point.
5. When all 400 squares are full, the game is over. If both players has the same amount of points the game ends in a tie. 6. The player with the most points wins.
